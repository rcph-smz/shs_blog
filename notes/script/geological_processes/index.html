geological processes
Earthquake
Earthquake is a sudden movement of ground along the faults lines when two blocks of the earth suddenly slip past one another (Plate boundaries are always faults, but not all faults are plate boundaries)
Most reasons earthquake happens is when: - Volcanic Eruption Occur, which creates Volcanic Earthquake - Plate tectonic boundaries slips, that could lead into Tectonic Earthquake - and Induced Earthquakes which is cause by human activity like tunnel construction and filling reservoirs
Earthquake could also cause some hazards like: - Ground rupture/surface fault - Ground Shaking - Liquefication - Tsunami, etc Just to name a few
Ground Shaking
Ground Shaking is a up, down and sideways vibration of the ground to break apart during an earthquake.
The hazards of ground shaking are mostly damaging or collapsing tall buildings ,it can also cause some landslides, mudslides, avalanches, melting or also known as Liquefication.
Surface faulting
Surface fautling is a Deformation of a ground that marks as a fault line on the earths surface
